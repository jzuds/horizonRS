# What are the types of flips we can capitalize on?

## Short Term Margin
- Signals: Price Margin
- Risk: Low
- Time: Minutes to hours
- Capital: Scales

## High Alchemy
- Signals: Buy Price vs Alch value
- Risk: Low
- Time: Slow - capped by alch speed
- Capital: Scales

## Processing / Conversion
- Signals: Component values
- Risk: Low
- Time: Slow - depedent on processing time
- Capital: Scales

## Quest / Diary Demand
- Signals: Content / New player count
- Risk: Low
- Time: Slow - quests are completed once by a player
- Capital: Low

## Supply Shock
- Signals: Demand Increase
- Risk: High
- Time: Opportunistic
- Capital: Scales

## Botting Dumps
- Signals: High volume + low price spike
- Risk: High
- Time: Daily to weekly
- Capital: Scales

## Update Speculation
- Signals: Update news
- Risk: Very High
- Time: Opportunistic
- Capital: Very High
